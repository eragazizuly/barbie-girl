Fashion Highlights

With the new millennium just around the corner, Generation Girl Barbie took on a new sassy, edgy, street-fashion look.

Collections

The “fab five” of the '90s now refers to Cindy (Crawford), Linda (Evangelista), Claudia (Schiffer), Naomi (Campbell) and Christy (Turlington) whose ultra-glam lifestyle reflected the pace of the decade’s race to the new millennium. The 1992 Totally Hair Barbie, the best-selling Barbie doll to date, celebrated the “big hair” craze and loud fashions of the early 90s. The doll featured the longest locks ever for Barbie, measuring 10½ inches.
